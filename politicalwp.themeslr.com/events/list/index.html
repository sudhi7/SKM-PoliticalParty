News/Events

Launch of DBU’s in India

Attended the launch of 75 Digital Banking Units (DBUs) in 75 districts of the country by the Hon'ble PM Shri Narendra Modi Ji via video conference. The DBUs are being dedicated to the nation in a view to ensure that the benefits of digital banking will reach every corner of the country. It will provide a variety of digital banking services and enable the customer to have convenient access and enhanced digital experience of banking facilities.

HCM meets Smt. Draupadi Murmu Ji

We had a fruitful meeting with the NDA presidential candidate Smt. Draupadi Murmu Ji in the presence of Hon’ble Union Minister Shri Sarbananda Sonowal Ji, Shri Gajendra Singh Shekhawat Ji & National Spokesperson of BJP Dr Sambit Patra Ji at Mayfair Hotel, Siliguri. As a part of the NDA, we expressed our commitment to wholeheartedly support her in the upcoming presidential election and collectively work for the welfare of Sikkim and our country.

State Level Entepreneurs meet held at Manan Kendra

Delighted to be a part of the State Level Entrepreneurs Meet at Manan Kendra. In addition to launching various products, cheques under the Skilled Youth Startup Scheme were handed out to the beneficiaries as assistance. The SEED cell is performing competently, serving as a bridge between the departments and the local entrepreneurs. We hope this initiative will benefit our aspiring entrepreneurs to establish themselves and be self-sufficient. Entrepreneurship can assist in economic growth, and our government is consistently encouraging and helping our youths and individuals in this regard.

SKM legislators meeting held at Mintokgang

Chaired the 14th edition of the SKM legislators meeting at Mintokgang in the presence of MLAs of SKM party, MP, Advisors, Political Secretary, and Press Secretary. The house unanimously passed a resolution to support and vote in favour of NDA candidate Smt. Draupadi Murmu in the Presidential election, scheduled for 18th July 2022. The strategies of the party for the forthcoming panchayat elections, tentatively scheduled for 2nd to 12th November 2022, were thoroughly discussed, followed by an update on the preparations for the upcoming Chalak Diwas. Similarly, we have decided to celebrate the Jan Unmukti Diwas 2022 in a grand manner. Collectively, we have also decided to hoist the National Flag in every house in Sikkim as a part of the "Har Ghar Tiranga" campaign, initiated by the Hon'ble PM Narendra Modi Ji to mark the 75th Independence day under Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav.

20th Foundation Day Celebration of the Press Club of Sikkim held at Manan Kendra

Delighted to attend the 20th Foundation Day Celebrations of the Press Club of Sikkim at Manan Kendra. I take this moment to congratulate the honorees for their contributions to the field of journalism in Sikkim. A free press & media is the cornerstone of democracy, and our government is fully committed to upholding the sanctity of journalism in the state and will extend every possible form of assistance for their welfare. The day is incomplete without acknowledging the impact of the press and media, and I urge them to collectively and proactively combat misinformation and disinformation in their pursuit to deliver news & data and exhibit the highest standards of professionalism in their duties.
