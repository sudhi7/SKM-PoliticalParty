About the Party

Sikkim Krantikari Morcha (SKM) was established on February 4, 2013 at Soreng, Sikkim.

SKM's headquarter is at Gangtok, Sikkim and its President Prem Singh Tamang alias P.S. Golay is a former member of the Sikkim Legislative Assembly and a Minister in the Government of Sikkim. He was one of the prominent figures in the previous ruling party, Sikkim Democratic Front (SDF). Prem Singh Tamang formally seceded from SDF, and became the Party President of SKM.

In 2017, SKM elected MLA Kunga Nima Lepcha as Acting President of the party and Similarly MP Subba as Working President. Party also appointed Arun Upreti as Secretary General of the party.
